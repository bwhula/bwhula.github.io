Console is King: An Investigation of Scalping of Gaming Related Hardware on the Second-Hand Market (May 17, 2021)

This project examines the second hand market for video game consoles and compute GPUs using data scraped from ebay in the Spring of 2021. It constitutes a tutorial of the data science pipeline in Python and utilizes tools such as pandas, numpy, sklearn, scipy, and proxycrawl. This project was originally completed as the final assignment for the CMSC320 course at the University of Maryland.
